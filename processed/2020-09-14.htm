Public hospitals daily update on COVID-19 cases
***********************************************
The following is issued on behalf of the Hospital Authority:

     As at 9am today (September 14), five COVID-19 confirmed patients were discharged from hospital in the last 24 hours. So far, a total of 4 635 patients with confirmed or probable infection have been discharged.
 
     At present, there are 670 negative pressure rooms in public hospitals with 1 198 negative pressure beds activated. A total of 194 confirmed patients are currently hospitalised in 19 public hospitals and a community treatment facility, among which 21 patients are in critical condition, eight are in serious condition and the remaining 165 patients are in stable condition.
 
     The Hospital Authority will maintain close contact with the Centre for Health Protection to monitor the latest developments and to inform the public and healthcare workers on the latest information in a timely manner.
