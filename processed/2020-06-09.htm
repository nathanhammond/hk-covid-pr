Public hospitals daily update on COVID-19 cases
***********************************************
The following is issued on behalf of the Hospital Authority:

     As at noon today (June 9), one COVID-19 confirmed patient (case number: 1049) was discharged from hospital in the past 24 hours. So far, a total of 1 050 patients with confirmed or probable infections have been discharged.
 
     At present, there are 636 negative pressure rooms in public hospitals with 1 188 negative pressure beds activated. A total of 54 confirmed patients are currently hospitalised in nine hospitals, among which three patients are in critical condition (case numbers: 595, 1084 and 1092), and the remaining 51 patients are in stable condition.
 
     The Hospital Authority will maintain close contact with the Centre for Health Protection to monitor the latest developments and to inform the public and healthcare workers on the latest information in a timely manner.
 
Ends/Tuesday, June 9, 2020
Issued at HKT 16:45
