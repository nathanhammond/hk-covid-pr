Public hospitals daily update on COVID-19 cases
***********************************************
The following is issued on behalf of the Hospital Authority:

     As at 9am today (September 12), 15 COVID-19 confirmed patients were discharged from hospital in the last 24 hours. So far, a total of 4 613 patients with confirmed or probable infection have been discharged.
      
     At present, there are 678 negative pressure rooms in public hospitals with 1 209 negative pressure beds activated. A total of 185 confirmed patients are currently hospitalised in 19 public hospitals and a community treatment facility, among which 21 patients are in critical condition, seven are in serious condition and the remaining 157 patients are in stable condition.
      
     The Hospital Authority will maintain close contact with the Centre for Health Protection to monitor the latest developments and to inform the public and healthcare workers on the latest information in a timely manner.
